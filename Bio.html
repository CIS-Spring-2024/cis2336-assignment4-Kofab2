Meet The Owner

Andrews Baah is the owner and oversees the ongoings at Cougar's Corner.

The owner of Cougar's Corner, with a passion for quality and hospitality, Andrews creates a haven where every dish tells a story of his hometown's flavors. Through his cafeteria, he fosters friendship, supporting local initiatives with warmth and dedication, inviting guests to savor the sweetness of life with every visit.
